History

Io was created in 2002 and actively developed until 2008 when became clear that maintaining a multi-platform build system and C implementation was too time consuming. A Javascript/WASM implementation may happen one day. Patches are still welcome.
Thanks to everyone that made contributions over the years, notably: Jonathan (Quag) Wright, Chris Double, Rich Collins, and Jeremy Tregunna. The mailing list archive and older darcs repo have more detailed info on Io's history.
